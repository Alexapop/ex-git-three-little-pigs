The three little pigs

Chapter 1 The Big Decision

Once upon a time, there were three little pigs who lived with their mother in a cozy cottage. One day, their mother said it was time for them to go out into the world and build houses on their own.

"Be careful," she warned. "Work hard, and always stay safe from the big bad wolf who lives in the woods."

The three little pigs packed their bags and set off on their big adventure, dreaming of the wonderful houses they would build.

Chapter 2 Straw, Sticks, and Bricks

The Straw House

The first little pig was very lazy. He decided to quickly build a house made of soft, golden straw so he could spend the rest of the day playing

Stick House

The second little pig worked a little harder. He gathered sticks from the forest and built a wooden house, which was a bit stronger than the straw one

The Brick House

The third little pig was very hardworking. He spent all day laying heavy red bricks with mortar to build a strong, sturdy house that could withstand anything.

Chapter 3 The Hungry Wolf

Soon, the Big Bad Wolf arrived. He went to the straw house and shouted in a deep, scary voice:

"Little pig, little pig, let me come in!"

"Not by the hair of my chinny-chin-chin!" squealed the pig. So the wolf huffed, and he puffed, and the BLEW THE HOUSE DOWN! The first little pig ran to the stick house. But the wolf follwed him and blew down that house too! Luckly, both pigs raced to their brother's strong brick house just in time.
