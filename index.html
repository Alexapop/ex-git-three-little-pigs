The three little pigs

Chapter 1 The Big Decision

Once upon a time, there were three little pigs who lived with their mother in a cozy cottage. One day, their mother said it was time for them to go out into the world and build houses on their own.

"Be careful," she warned. "Work hard, and always stay safe from the big bad wolf who lives in the woods."

The three little pigs packed their bags and set off on their big adventure, dreaming of the wonderful houses they would build.
